HOW TO MEASURE

Use a soft measuring tape. Measure over light clothing or directly on skin for best accuracy.

1
Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
2
Length
From the highest point of the shoulder down to the hem of the garment.
3
Shoulder
From one shoulder seam to the other, measured across the back.
4
Sleeve
From the shoulder seam down to the cuff. For hoodies and longsleeved, measure from shoulder tip to wrist.
5
Waist / Hip
For pants — waist at the narrowest point, hip at the fullest part. Keep tape snug but not tight.

📏
Between Sizes?

If your measurements fall between two sizes, go with the larger one for the intended oversized fit — or the smaller for a more structured look.

🧺
After Washing

Wash cold, inside out, on a gentle cycle. Hang dry or tumble low. Avoid bleach. Proper care keeps graphics sharp and fabric soft longer.
