Common Questions
How long does a refund take to appear?
+

Once approved, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment provider — GCash and Maya are typically instant, while card refunds can take 5–10 business days.

Do I need the original packaging to return an item?
+

Original packaging is preferred but not required. What matters is that the item is packed securely enough not to be damaged in transit, and that your return reference number is included inside the parcel.

Can I return an item bought during a sale or promo?
+

Sale items are eligible for return only if they arrive defective, damaged, or are the wrong item. Change-of-mind returns are not accepted on discounted or promotional items.

What if my item was lost in transit?
+

If your tracking shows no movement for more than 7 days, or if your package is marked delivered but not received, contact us within 48 hours. We'll investigate with the courier and issue a full refund or reship at no cost to you.

How do I track my return shipment?
+

We recommend using a tracked courier when sending your return. Keep the tracking number and send it to us so we can monitor the shipment and process your refund as soon as it arrives.
