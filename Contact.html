Quick Answers

COMMON QUESTIONS

What products do you sell?
+

We sell premium gymwear and streetwear from the breathdivinity × Ascend collaboration — hoodies, tees, longsleeved shirts, sweatpants, tanks, and more. All clothing, no supplements or equipment.

Do you offer nationwide delivery?
+

Yes — we deliver nationwide across the Philippines. Orders are dispatched within 24 hours from Manila and arrive within 3–5 business days. Free shipping on orders over ₱2,000.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), GCash, Maya, and all major credit and debit cards. Your payment is always secure.

What is your return policy?
+

We offer a 30-day return policy for any defective or damaged items. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Contact us to start a return.

How do I choose the right size?
+

Our clothing runs in sizes XS to 3XL. All items are oversized or streetwear-fit — we recommend sizing down if you prefer a more fitted look. When in doubt, contact us for a size guide.

What is the breathdivinity × Ascend collab?
+

breathdivinity is a US-based gothic streetwear brand. Ascend brings their pieces directly to Filipino athletes and streetwear fans — from the USA, straight to PH 🇺🇸→🇵🇭.
