ORDERS6 Articles
How do I place an order?
+

Browse our shop and add items to your cart using the + Add to Cart button. When you're ready, open the cart drawer and click Proceed to Checkout →. Follow the 4 steps: cart review → shipping details → payment method → confirm order. You'll receive a confirmation with your order number immediately after.

Can I modify or cancel my order?
+

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of being placed, before they enter packing. Contact us immediately via the contact page with your order number. Once an order is packed or dispatched, it cannot be cancelled and must go through our returns process.

How do I track my order?
+

Once your order is dispatched — usually within 24 hours of payment — you'll receive a tracking number via email or SMS. Use this number on your courier's website or app to track in real time. If you haven't received a tracking number after 2 business days, contact us and we'll look into it right away.

What is my order confirmation number?
+

Your order confirmation number follows the format ASC-XXXXXX (e.g. ASC-482931). It's shown on the checkout success screen and sent to your email. Keep this number for tracking, returns, and any support queries — it's how we identify your order in our system.

Can I add items to an existing order?
+

Unfortunately, we're unable to add items to an existing order once it has been placed. You'll need to place a new separate order. If your new order qualifies for free shipping (over ₱2,000 total), the discount will apply automatically at checkout.

What if an item I ordered is out of stock?
+

In the rare event that an item becomes unavailable after your order is placed, we'll contact you via email within 24 hours to offer either a full refund for that item or an alternative of similar value. The rest of your order will be fulfilled as normal.

SHIPPING5 Articles
How long does delivery take?
+

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days nationwide. Metro Manila orders typically arrive in 1–3 days. We also offer Express Delivery for Metro Manila orders placed before 10AM — these arrive same day or next day. See our full shipping guide for regional estimates.

How much does shipping cost?
+

Standard shipping costs ₱99–₱149 depending on your region. Orders totaling ₱2,000 or more automatically qualify for free shipping — no promo code needed, it's applied at checkout. Express delivery for Metro Manila is ₱199–₱249.

Do you ship nationwide in the Philippines?
+

Yes — we ship to all regions of the Philippines including Metro Manila, Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Some remote island areas (Visayas Islands, parts of BARMM) have limited courier coverage and may take longer. Check the regional delivery table for specifics.

My package says delivered but I didn't receive it.
+

Contact us within 48 hours of the marked delivery time via the contact page with your order number and tracking details. We'll file a trace with the courier and, if the package cannot be located within 5 business days, we'll issue a full refund or reship at no cost.

How are orders packaged?
+

Every item is carefully folded flat and placed inside a sealed poly mailer with a branded inner wrap to protect the graphic prints during transit. Sets and multi-item orders are packed together in a single parcel unless otherwise noted.

RETURNS & REFUNDS5 Articles
What is your return policy?
+

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for: defective or damaged items, wrong item sent, and size exchanges (1× free per order). Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have original tags. Change of mind returns are not accepted. Visit the Returns page to submit a request.

How do I start a return?
+

Go to our Returns page and fill in the return request form. You'll need your order number, item details, reason for return, and photos of any defect. We'll review within 1–2 business days and email you with approval and shipping instructions.

How long does a refund take?
+

After we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. GCash and Maya refunds are typically instant once processed. Card refunds may take an additional 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

Can I exchange for a different size?
+

Yes — we offer 1 free size exchange per order within 30 days. The item must be unworn, unwashed, and have its original tags attached. Customer pays return shipping; we cover the new item's delivery. Submit via the Returns page and select "Exchange — Different Size."

My item arrived damaged — what do I do?
+

We're sorry about that. Damaged or defective items qualify for a full refund or free replacement, and we'll cover all return shipping. Go to the Returns page, select "Defective / Damaged" as the reason, and attach clear photos. We'll resolve it fast.

SIZING4 Articles
What sizes do you carry?
+

All breathdivinity pieces are available in XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL, and 3XL. M and L are our most popular sizes. See the size guide for detailed measurements in both CM and inches across all categories — Tees, Hoodies, Longsleeved, Sweatpants, and Tanks.

How do the clothes fit?
+

All breathdivinity pieces are cut oversized and streetwear-fit by design — dropped shoulders, relaxed chest, and longer length. If you prefer a more structured, fitted silhouette, we recommend sizing down by one size. When in doubt, message us with your measurements.

How do I measure myself correctly?
+

Use a soft measuring tape: Chest — wrap around the fullest part, parallel to the floor. Shoulder — across your back from seam to seam. Length — from highest shoulder point to hem. Waist / Hip — for sweatpants, measure at the narrowest and fullest points respectively. Full guide at sizing-guide.html.

I'm between two sizes — which should I pick?
+

If you're between sizes, pick the larger size for the oversized streetwear look the collection is designed for, or the smaller size if you want it more fitted. Still unsure? Message us on the contact page with your chest and shoulder measurements and we'll recommend the best size.

PAYMENT5 Articles
What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), GCash, Maya, and all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard). Select your preferred method at checkout — no account needed for COD. GCash and Maya payment numbers are sent in your order confirmation email.

How do I use a promo code?
+

On the checkout page, enter your promo code in the promo code field in the order summary panel and click Apply. Valid codes: ASCEND10, BD10, JUSTASCEND, BREATHDIVINITY — all give 10% off your order total. Only one promo code can be applied per order.

Is Cash on Delivery available everywhere?
+

COD is available in most areas across the Philippines, including Metro Manila, major cities in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Some remote island areas and parts of BARMM may not support COD. Availability will be confirmed at checkout based on your delivery address.

My payment failed — what should I do?
+

If your card payment fails, check with your bank that the card is enabled for online transactions. Try a different card or switch to GCash or Maya. Your order will not be placed until payment is successfully confirmed. If you're still having issues, contact us and we'll help manually.

Is it safe to pay online on Ascend?
+

Yes — all card payments are processed through a secure encrypted payment gateway. We never store your card details on our servers. GCash and Maya payments are also processed through their own secure platforms. Your payment information is always safe with us.

ACCOUNT4 Articles
Do I need an account to shop?
+

No — you can shop and check out as a guest without creating an account. However, creating a free account gives you faster checkout, order history access, and early notifications for new drops. Create one at login.html.

How do I create an account?
+

Visit our login page and click Create Account. Enter your first and last name, email address, and a password (minimum 8 characters). You'll be logged in instantly. You can optionally opt in to receive early access to new drops and exclusive offers.

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?
+

On the login page, click Forgot password? below the password field. Enter your registered email address and we'll send a password reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

How do I manage marketing emails?
+

During registration, you can tick the checkbox to receive early access to drops and exclusive offers. To opt out at any time, simply contact us with your email address and we'll remove you from our marketing list within 2 business days. Order confirmation emails cannot be unsubscribed from.

PRODUCTS4 Articles
How should I wash my breathdivinity pieces?
+

To keep your pieces looking sharp: Machine wash cold, inside out, gentle cycle. Hang dry or tumble dry on low heat. Do not bleach. Do not iron directly on printed areas — use a cloth between the iron and print. Following these instructions keeps graphics crisp and fabric quality intact.

What is the breathdivinity × Ascend collab?
+

breathdivinity is a gothic streetwear brand born in the streets of the USA, known for bold skull graphics, oversized silhouettes, and dark aesthetic. Ascend is the exclusive Philippine partner — bringing authentic breathdivinity pieces directly to Filipino athletes and streetwear fans. From the USA, straight to PH. 🇺🇸→🇵🇭

What are the clothes made of?
+

breathdivinity uses premium heavyweight cotton blends across all categories. Hoodies and longsleeved pieces are heavier weight for durability and structure. Tees and tanks use mid-weight cotton for comfort and breathability. Exact fabric composition varies by piece — message us for specific details on an item.

Will sold-out items be restocked?
+

Yes — popular items are restocked periodically depending on availability from breathdivinity USA. To be notified when a specific item returns, contact us and let us know which piece and size you're waiting for. We'll reach out as soon as stock is available.
